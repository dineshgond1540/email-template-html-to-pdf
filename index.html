Tour Itinerary
Day 1: Arrival Indore- Ujjain (77 kms - 2 hrs)

Arrive at Indore. proceed to Ujjain. on arrival check into hotel, later proceed for Half day sightseeing at Ujjain. One of most holy cities in the Hindu religion and one of site host the kumbha Mela. Sightseeing start from Mahakaleshwar temple one of the 12 Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva. later visit Iskon temple, Shakti Peet, Harsiddhi Temple, Sandipani Ashram, Bade Ganesh Ka Mandir and Ram Ghat etc. Evening attend arti at Mahakaleshwar temple is the main attraction. Overnight stay at hote

Note: - Attending Bhasm arti of Mahakaleshwar temple is the prime attraction of Ujjain visit. This arti remains in demand so need to be booked in advance. Arti is offered in morning & evening hours at the temple. Overnight stay at hotel
